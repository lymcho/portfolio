Why bookable marketplace?


THE PROBLEM

Customers love discounts. But they also need to be convenient. How Many people bought a groupon deal then never got around to use it? In the long run, that’s not good customers and not good for Groupon, because people won’t buy a new deal until they’ve used the previous one. That’s why need Groupon to be Bookable. We want convenience, quality and loyalty. Merchants hate discounts. But they have to give discounts in exchange for exposure. How many merchants are willing offers a 50 to 75 percent discount without going out of business? It’s not an infinite list. To increase inventory, we need to be a connect marketplace, a business model that allows merchants to sell on Groupon with low or no discounts.


THE DESIGN GOAL

We are focused on not only delivering the best consumer experience we can but also all the other parts behind the hood what makes booking possible.
