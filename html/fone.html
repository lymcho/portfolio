KidFone

Parent-controlled kids'phone device


DATE

2017


CLIENT

Fone


Role

Branding, UI/UX



The Brief

In this project, I worked with the client to visualize his product idea. His initial ask was for me to design an UI for a 320x280 touch-screen phone, with a parent-controlled address book.

Initial Definitions

In our second meeting, I presented a sitemap that captured his requirements for the kids interface and also an "expansion pack" - a scope for the parent-facing interface.

The Concept

Kid's Interface

My idea was to gamify the experience for kids - they can add their friends to the contact list but will only have a fixed amount of calling time per friend per week. This way, while parents still have the control they need, kids can learn to manage time better.
