In this approach, I created a linetype("liftlabs") - it's used throughout these compositions as a structural element, symbolizing liftlab's supportive nature. The yellow elevator is little fun brand element taken from the literal meaning of "lift".






Concept 2

This approach goes after the theme of "movie posters". The bold and rounded san serif font emphasizes the "movie" look & feel. It's laid out on top of abstract architectural material surfaces which suggests the "structure & support" nature of the liftlabs. These poster concepts were part of my visual explorations centered around the three main themes: "media" - NBC, "connectivity" -Comcast and "entertainment" - Universal.
