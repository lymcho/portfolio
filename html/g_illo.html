Groupon Illustration System

SUMMARY

In the beginning of 2021, Groupon launched a global marketing campaign. it showcases Groupon as the go-to destination for local experiences. Followed by the campaign launch, I engaged and then collaborated with both the brand team to discuss how illustrations could fit in our new branding initiative.


DATE

2021


COMPANY

Groupon


Role

Senior Product Designer, Illustrator




The Context


THE OPPORTUNITY

The campaign launch left us a big shoe to fill - how should we approach the new brand in the product context.

Being the illustration expert on the team, I started putting formal thoughts towards an illustration system as part of the on-going new campaign push.


Illustrations, just like all of the other brand elements, represent Groupon's tone and voice, and also our principals and missions - we advocate for exciting experiences, being inclusive, and being human, and our illustration style should reflect that.

The Challenges

There are so many Illustration styles out there and there are many different directions we could go towards. The direction I took the team towards was that we should be both bold and experimental while also factoring in flexibility and practicality.

Example Usecases

One of the most recent application of my illustration are the category icons on Groupon's home page.

This illustrated approach, won the a/b test experiment in native platforms, which spoke for value of delightful illustrations in otherwise purely functional user experience.
