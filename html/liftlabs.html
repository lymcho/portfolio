Comcast/NBC Universal

Exploring the look & feel of a brand-new innovation powerhouse


SUMMARY

This was a branding project I worked on at VV for Comcast/NBCUniversal's co-branded entrepreneurship hub called liftlabs. I developed a series of visual concepts as a part of our moodboard presentation.


DATE

2017-present


Role

Visual Design, Branding



Concept 1

In this approach, I created a linetype("liftlabs") - it's used throughout these compositions as a structural element, symbolizing liftlab's supportive nature. The yellow elevator is little fun brand element taken from the literal meaning of "lift".

I played with a variety of layouts (poster & brochure etc) to suggest the different ways this concept can be used. In the poster, the fluid colorful blurbs exist to break the line-heavy layout as well as carry through comcast's brand colors.




Since the client also responded really well to the geometric lettertype in the moodboard, I continued to explore in that direction.

In these examples, I refined the typography with detailes and laid them on an isometric base. I built each letter using this style and this allowed me to play with their relationship to each other and to the space they inhabit. What I liked the most about this style is its spacial dynamics, which I think reflects Liftlab's brand voice in a really sophisticated yet unexpected way.



Concept 2

This approach goes after the theme of "movie posters". The bold and rounded san serif font emphasizes the "movie" look & feel. It's laid out on top of abstract architectural material surfaces which suggests the "structure & support" nature of the liftlabs.






These are three select posters addressing the three main themes of liftlab's entrepreneurial targets: "media" - NBC, "connectivity" -Comcast and "entertainment" - Universal.





I prepared these as the continuouation of the movie posters.

The part "liftlabs" is a consistent graphic element in each one of the poster. It consists of 3 parts: the "lif" leverages use of photography to express a variety of mood; the "labs" grounds the composition with a solid color(black) lockup; a uplifting thin line with a five color gradient that represents the Comcast/NBCU brand.
